The sound track "Runaround" was created by Yubatake and downloaded from https://opengameart.org/

This sound track was modified by RX1 & RuJo in following ways:
	- normalized and converted to ogg format
	- shortened to make it loopable in SuperTuxKart
	- additional creation of an 8 % faster version (for the final race lap)

Files:
	Yubatake_-_Runaround_(loopable).ogg
	Yubatake_-_Runaround_(fast_loopable).ogg
